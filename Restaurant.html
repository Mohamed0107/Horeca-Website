Wall-Stock 61 offers a perfect combination of classic and modern
world cuisines with a splash of local flavor. It’s a unique place,
welcoming local and international crowd; only here you can play a
billiard from 1935 while listening to the soft sounds of a vinyl player.
Bring your kids along, they won’t get bored at the ‘kids corner’ full of
activities.

Enjoy the vibrant atmosphere at the cozy indoors or at our outdoor
riverside terrace along the Maas. Don’t forget to check out our great
selection of special beers. Perhaps fancy a glass of cold Guinness?
